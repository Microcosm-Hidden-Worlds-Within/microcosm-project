Hidden Worlds Within

Explore the exhibition

What is about?

Explore Microcosm: Hidden Worlds Within where science meets art through stunning micrographs of the human body. This exhibition reveals the intricate complexity and elegance of human tissues and cells, showcasing the extraordinary beauty beneath the surface.

Join us for a visual journey that celebrates the marvels of human biology in vivid colors and delicate structures.

Narratives

Circulatory system

It moves blood, delivering oxygen and nutrients while removing waste.

Digestive system

It breaks down food into nutrients and eliminates waste.

Immune system

It defends against infections and diseases by identifying and destroying harmful invaders.

Integumentary system

It includes the skin, hair, and nails, providing protection and helping regulate temperature.

Muscular system

It enables movement and maintains posture through muscle contractions.

Nervous system

It controls and coordinates activities by transmitting signals to and from different parts of the body.

Respiratory system

It facilitates breathing, taking in oxygen and expelling carbon dioxide.

Skeletal system

It provides structure, protects organs, and supports movement.

Urinary system

It removes waste from the blood and regulates water and electrolyte balance.
